Our Mission

ArtSonix is designed to bridge the worlds of visual and auditory art, creating a synesthetic experience that enhances appreciation for both mediums. We believe that art and music are intrinsically connected, and when experienced together, they can unlock new dimensions of enjoyment and understanding.

How It Works

2

3

Experience your personalized selection of artwork and music that complement each other.

Our Data Sources

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

We utilize The Met's open access API to bring you artwork from one of the world's most prestigious art collections. With over 5,000 years of art from around the globe, The Met's collection offers unprecedented breadth and depth.

Visit The Met's Website

Spotify

Spotify's comprehensive music API allows us to recommend music that perfectly complements your selected artwork. With millions of tracks across all genres, we can find music that resonates with the visual art you're experiencing.

Visit Spotify's Website

Privacy & Data Use

At ArtSonix, we respect your privacy. We don't store any personal information or preferences beyond your current session. Your selections are used only to generate recommendations and are not shared with third parties.
